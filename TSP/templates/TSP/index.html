About Advitiya

IIT Ropar is organizing the fourth edition of it's annual Science & Technology Festival "Advitiya" in the first week of of February consisting of 72 hours of intense competitions and technical events. Advitiya has already evolved into a brand within four years of its inception. Advitiya achieved more than 15k footfall and conducted 30+ events in its previous edition. Advitiya, in its past few editions, not only has created a buzz in the minds of Techno-enthusiasts but has also provided its partners and sponsors a platform for their publicity. With an array of activities that consolidate the vision for times to come, Advitiya 2020 is ready to set the scientific spirits soaring and technological temper ablaze. Advitiya will witness a wide range of scientific and technical activities, and novel events from fields like Coding, Aeromodelling, Robotics, Finance, Design and Entrepreneurship with total prizes worth as high as 9 lakh Rupees.
