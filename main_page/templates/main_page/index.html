About Advitiya

Welcome to IIT Ropar's Technical FEst

IIT Ropar is organizing the fourth edition of it's annual Technical Festival "Advitiya" in the first week of of February consisting of 72 hours of intense competitions and technical events Advitiya has already evolved into a brand within four years of its inception and a publicity paradise that ensures stupendous brand visibility for sponsors With an array of activities that consolidate the vision for times to come, Advitiya 2020 is ready to set the scientific spirits soaring and technological temper ablaze Advitiya will witness a wide range of scientific and technical activities and novel events from fields like Coding, Robotics, Finance, Design and Entrepreneurship with total prizes worth as high as 9 lakh Rupees.

IIT's are not only the technological hubs of their regions but are also known for amalgamating education with fun-filled extra-curricular activities.In this regard the IIT Ropar organises its science and technology fest Advitiya whose third edition was organised last year. Continuing with that tradition; with immense pleasure and pride we please to announce Advitiya 2020.


Dr. C C Reddy

Dean Student Affairs

Advitiya, inspires students to come out of their regular routine curriculum, to think innovatively, to brainstorm ideas, to accept challenges and to create new products. Aiming to be one of the finest Science and Technology fest of North India, Advitiya provides a superlative platform where students from all parts of the nation compete against each other.


Dr. Neeraj Goel

Faculty Advisor

In this edition of Advitiya, we plan to host workshops on latest technological trends, motivational talks, exhibitions, technical competitions such as coding, designing, robotics, business plan, quizzes, hackathon and many more. Overall three days of intensely loaded schedule, which will give the students a platform to showcase their imagination, skills, and innovativeness.

Mr. Ashish Bedi

General Secretary, BOST
