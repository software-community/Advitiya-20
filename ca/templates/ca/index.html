About Advitiya

IIT Ropar is organizing the fourth edition of it's annual Science & Technology Festival "Advitiya" in the first week of February consisting of 72 hours of exhilarating competitions and technical events. Advitiya has already evolved into a brand within four years of its inception. In the previous edition, we managed to record a footfall of 15k+ and hosted over 30 events. Advitiya, in its past few editions, not only has created a buzz in the minds of Techno-enthusiasts but has also provided its partners and sponsors a platform for their publicity. With an array of activities that consolidate the vision for times to come, Advitiya 2020 is ready to set the scientific spirits soaring and technological temper ablaze.
