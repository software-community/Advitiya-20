About TechConnect

Techconnect is a pre-festival zonal event which comprises of preliminary rounds of some major competitions of Advitiya. Techconnect comprises of engaging professional technical workshops to enhance the learning of students and to encourage the zeal of the participants. Techconnect covers all the major cities of India and it witnessed a participation of around 2000 in workshops and events from nearly 40+ colleges previous year.

Techconnect is a highly publicized event that is mentioned in our posters, banners, social media pages and in the website of Advitiya, IIT Ropar. Association with Techconnect will help your institute garner positive publicity and it reach a wider audience all across the span of the country.
